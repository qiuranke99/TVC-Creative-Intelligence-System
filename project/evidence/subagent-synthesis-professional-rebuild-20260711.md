# TCIS V3 Professional Architecture Synthesis

Date: 2026-07-11
Status: current architecture evidence
Scope: professional advertising operating model, roles, decision authority, and failure boundaries

## Synthesis verdict

TCIS V3 must operate as a professional advertising organization with a deterministic
engineering substrate. The engineering layer can protect evidence, state, versions,
dependencies, actual-media lineage, recovery, and delivery, but it cannot replace
strategy, creative judgment, craft ownership, or human release authority.

The architecture is accepted for specification use under the following non-compensable
conditions:

- strategy and the sharp creative brief precede agency creative development;
- Copywriter and Agency Art Director develop ideas together under Creative Director leadership;
- one-off films are not forced through a campaign-platform stage;
- production-company interpretation does not pre-empt agency concept, script, and board;
- agency, production-company, editorial, and post-production roles retain distinct authority;
- every decision-bearing artifact returns through human advice, AI revision, and explicit lock or reopen;
- claims, rights, feasibility, professional signoff, and release remain independent;
- prompt success and schema completion never stand in for inspection of actual media.

## Professional route

The default route is:

1. client brief and immersion;
2. diagnosis and communications strategy;
3. sharp creative brief;
4. Copywriter/Agency Art Director paired development with CD critique and incubation;
5. core creative decision and TVC film expression;
6. script, agency board, and claim review;
7. agency visual predevelopment;
8. production pitch, director treatment, and award;
9. PPM and execution planning;
10. shoot or generation;
11. offline, finish, and release.

Alternative production modes may change entry timing, but every exception must declare
scope, authority, IP, fee, approval, and handoff consequences.

## Decision model

Every formal artifact that changes objective, meaning, creative direction, taste, risk,
budget, production path, or release consequence follows:

```text
AI proposals -> human advice/selection -> AI revision -> human lock/reopen
```

First selection is not lock. Silence is not approval. `none` and `reopen` remain valid.
Technical verification stays automatic unless it reveals a decision-bearing consequence.

## Role boundaries

- Creative Director owns the agency creative standard; Commercial Director owns awarded production interpretation.
- Agency Art Director owns agency visual meaning; Production Designer owns the physical or generated world for production.
- Agency Producer owns agency-side delivery and approval coordination; Production-company Producer owns execution feasibility and delivery.
- Editor owns editorial construction; Post Producer owns post schedule, vendors, resources, and delivery coordination.
- Claims and rights owners may stop release without becoming creative owners.

## Platform applicability

`creative platform` is conditional. It is justified only when the work needs a shared,
generative mechanism across multiple executions, channels, waves, markets, or a durable
brand system. A single TVC normally requires a strong film concept or advertising idea,
not an invented platform layer.

## Evidence and failure boundary

Professional sources establish role logic and methods, not guaranteed outcomes. Runtime
tests establish bounded system behavior, not creative or commercial quality. Any fatal
role-authority error, unsupported claim promotion, cross-client contamination, silent
decision bypass, or fake media success is a non-compensable NO-GO.
