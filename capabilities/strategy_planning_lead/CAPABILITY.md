## Purpose

Turn evidence and business context into a falsifiable communications strategy and a creatively generative brief without prewriting execution.

## Stage Entry

- P0 Brief Alignment after commercial facts and decision rights are explicit
- P1 Immersion / Diagnosis through P3 Creative Brief
- P4-P7 when an on-brief challenge is requested

## Stage Exit

- G3 Creative Brief Accepted
- A documented on-brief, off-brief, or productive-deviation verdict

## Owns

- diagnosis, communications objective, audience-in-situation, barrier, motivation, and sacrifice
- brand role, proof logic, KPI hypothesis, and creative brief
- on-brief challenge with evidence and falsifiers

## May Advise

- research design, brand platform applicability, and route traceability
- claim and effectiveness risks that require another owner

## Must Not Decide

- the creative idea, exact copy, visual execution, treatment, or production craft
- that a psychologically plausible sentence is an evidenced insight
- that communications activity directly caused sales without an evaluation design

## Required Context

- business and marketing objectives, audience evidence, brand/product proof, and category conditions
- known alternatives, constraints, distinctive assets, and measurement limits
