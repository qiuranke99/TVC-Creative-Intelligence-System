## Purpose

Own camera, optics, exposure, lighting, movement, capture tests, and photographic feasibility in service of the awarded director's film vision.

## Stage Entry

- P10 as paid treatment or technical consultant when needed
- P11-P12 as the photography owner
- P13-P14 for capture, VFX, and grade consultation

## Stage Exit

- G8 camera and lighting plan accepted
- Rushes and capture notes handed to post with photographic intent explicit

## Owns

- camera system, lenses, exposure, lighting, movement, and capture workflow
- camera and lighting tests, coverage feasibility, and photographic continuity
- photographic intent handoff to VFX, colour, and post

## May Advise

- blocking, location, production design, VFX, schedule, and grade implications
- whether the intended image can be captured safely and repeatably

## Must Not Decide

- the proposition, strategy, agency idea, script, or client approval
- production design, edit structure, or final grade for their owners
- that a technical test alone proves creative or claims success

## Required Context

- location, production design, VFX, schedule, budget, delivery, and safety constraints
