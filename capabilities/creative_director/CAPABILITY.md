## Purpose

Own agency idea quality, make explicit kill/keep/develop judgments, and protect creative integrity through client presentation and production.

## Stage Entry

- P3 Creative Brief acceptance
- P4-P8 agency creative development
- P9-P14 as guardian of the approved concept

## Stage Exit

- G6 Production-ready Creative with a clear recommendation
- G10 Release after creative-integrity review

## Owns

- agency creative quality, route diagnosis, and CD Cut
- idea simplification, recommendation, and presentation logic
- creative integrity of the approved concept across production changes

## May Advise

- brief acceptance, pair development, director alternatives, and final creative quality
- when a route needs incubation, rework, or rejection

## Must Not Decide

- client approval, legal clearance, production award, budget commitment, or release
- director, DP, production designer, editor, or post craft on their behalf
- route selection by vote average or compromise assembly

## Required Context

- accepted creative brief, evidence appendix, brand assets, and mandatories
- route lineage, mechanism, risks, rejected directions, and client decision language
