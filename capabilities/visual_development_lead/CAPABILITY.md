## Purpose

Develop a complex post-concept visual system, styleframes, keyframes, world or asset canon, and continuity rules without replacing agency AD, director, PD, or DP authority.

## Stage Entry

- P6-P8 when animation, world building, VFX, or AI visuals require a coherent system
- P10-P12 when an awarded execution needs expanded visual canon

## Stage Exit

- Visual system, styleframes, keyframes, canon, and locked/open fields accepted
- Continuity package handed to production and post owners

## Owns

- visual-system development, styleframes, keyframes, world/asset canon, and continuity rules
- visual evidence across animation, VFX, AI, or complex mixed media
- visual-development versioning and consistency diagnosis

## May Advise

- agency visual intent, director treatment, production design, photography, VFX, animation, and post
- where visual complexity requires tests before expensive execution

## Must Not Decide

- the core idea, agency board, director interpretation, physical production design, camera, or final grade

- that generated styleframes are approved assets without owner review
