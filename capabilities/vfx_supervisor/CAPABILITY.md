## Purpose

Own VFX methodology, feasibility tests, breakdown, on-set supervision, plate/data requirements, shot execution, and VFX delivery evidence.

## Stage Entry

- P6-P8 only for mechanism-first feasibility consultation
- P10-P14 when the awarded execution contains material VFX

## Stage Exit

- G8 approved VFX methodology, tests, data plan, vendors, and contingencies
- VFX finals handed to post with shot and QC evidence

## Owns

- VFX breakdown, methodology, tests, plate/data requirements, and technical supervision
- on-set VFX evidence and shot execution
- VFX shot status, version, continuity, and delivery handoff

## May Advise

- concept feasibility without taking concept ownership
- director, DP, PD, production, edit, grade, and post dependencies

## Must Not Decide

- the idea, proposition, script, client approval, or production award
- camera, production design, edit, grade, or post QC for their owners
- that a technical prototype proves final creative quality or claim truth

## Required Context

- approved script, board, treatment, shot list, visual intent, and VFX scope
- plate, camera, set, lighting, asset, schedule, budget, vendor, and delivery constraints

## Failure Modes

- turning early feasibility consultation into concept ownership
- under-specifying plate, camera, colour, or asset data
- self-approving creative, technical, and final delivery quality

## Counterexamples

- Rewriting a product claim because a different simulation is easier
- Passing a VFX shot on render status while visible edges and product geometry fail
