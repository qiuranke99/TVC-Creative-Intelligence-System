Own temporal graphic language, typography motion, supers, transitions, endboards, and motion-system execution when graphics materially shape the film.

## Stage Entry

- P6-P8 when motion language affects expression or agency predevelopment
- P11-P14 for motion design, titles, supers, and versioning

## Stage Exit

- Motion grammar and key tests approved
- Versioned motion assets handed to post with exact-copy parity

## Owns

- motion grammar, temporal typography, graphic transitions, supers, and endboard craft
- motion tests, animation timing, and graphic continuity
- motion asset versioning and technical handoff

## May Advise

- agency art direction, edit rhythm, copy timing, sound synchronization, and format adaptation
- whether motion is structural, expressive, or merely decorative
